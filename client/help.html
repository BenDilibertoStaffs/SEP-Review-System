Introduction

Hello! This guide will take show you how to use the system. It will detail sign up, login, sign out, adding a business, searching for a restaurant, adding a restaurant.

To login:

  • Go to the top navigation of the page
  • Click on account actions
  • Click on Login
  • Enter your username between 1 and 20 characters
  • Enter your password in a text box between 1 and 20 characters
  • Press submit. If successful, the pop will tell you

To Sign up:

  • Go to the top navigation of the page
  • Click on account actions
  • Click on Sign up
  • Enter your username between 1 and 20 characters
  • Enter your password in a text box between 1 and 20 characters
  • Renter your password in a text box between 1 and 20 characters
  • Press submit. If successful, the pop will tell you

To sign out:

  • Go to the top navigation of the page
  • Click on account ac tions

To add a business:

  • Go to the top navigation of the page
  • Click on account actions
